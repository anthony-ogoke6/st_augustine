ABOUT US - THE RETURN OF MISSIONARIES - 2002.

The Claretian Missionaries were the second missionaries to work in Ikorodu after the white missionaries. The Claretians, Rev. Fr. Henry Omonisaye, CMF was made the Parish Priest while Rev. Fr. Daniel Mary Onyeayana, CMF and Rev. Fr. Philip Igbo, CMF were his co-pastors respectively.

In 2002, Rev. Fr. Philip Igbo, CMF left for further studies in Rome and Rev. Fr. Augustine Amadi, CMF replaced him.

On the 1st of December 2008, Rev. Fr. Daniel Mary Onyeayana, CMF left for mission in the U.S.A and this ushered in Rev. Fr. Augustine Medaiyedu-Ambrose, CMF as the new Parish Pastor with Rev. Jude Dominic Adesina Igba, CMF as his Co-Pastor in March 2020.

In September 2016, Frs. Augustine Medaiyedu-Ambrose and Mike Ekeh, CMf were transferred respectively. This development ushered in Rev. Fr. Livinus Ikechukeu Orakwe, CMf as the Parish Pastor on 30th september 2016 with Rev. Fr. Adolphud Mbaocha and John Omoha CMF as Co-Pastors.

Within a space of time of their stay in the parish, they have made very remarkable impressions on the church. Notable among them is the creation of St. Anthony Mary Claret Catholic Church, Eyita (outstation) arid Immaculate Heart Catholic Church, Maya and Our Lady of Fatima Adamo and the construction of the parish warehouse, archive were realized. The establishment of a unified Saturday Devotion cannot but be mentioned. They have also produced and mounted a Giant Statue of the Patron Saint of the Parish, St. Augustine's of Hippo. A Construction of an Ultra-Modern Toilet Facility named after Very Rev. Msgr. Sylvester E.U. Osigweh. In the year 2018 Our Lady of Fatima Catholic Church, was established at Adamo, Ikorodu. An extension has also been made to accommodate the Catechism Classes and Confessional. The construction of the CCM Pieta was done and the erection security street lights were also realized. The renovation of Marian Grotto, the installation of the digital screen in front of the church and the creation of the Harvest/PFC office were made. The Parish bookshop was expanded while the business center/ PFC data point was again realized. The Parish uniform and the establishment of the Patron’s novena booklet were also added. Also the construction of New Church Building at Imota and Itamope’s father;s House.

At the time security deficit was ravaging the country, the two major gates were permanented for entrance while the other for exit in -order to ease movement. A Parish Car wash was inaugurated in December, 1019n12 pat of Empowerment Programme by the Mission Statement Committee.

In 2018 the Archbishop OF Lagos carved out St. Andrew’s Catholic Church, Itamope as a Quasi Parish with the Priest-in-Charge, Rev. FR. Kevin Okuma. She left with A S Imota IHM Maya, St. Daniel Isiu and OLF Adamo. In all of the growth and development of the life of the Faithful is not undermined.

The achievement recorded above have all been motivated by progressive growth in the spiritual life of the faithful of the Parish and the continuous generosity, support and contributions of the parishioners and the spirit-pastoral encouragement by the chains of priests who worked in Ikorodu including those who are still working in the Parish till now, in August 2019, Frs. John Omoha Cmf and Adolphus Mbaocha CMf were transferred while Frs. Moses Okpanach Musa CMF, Louis Ajinge CMf and Emmanuel Ubi Terseer CMF were posted to replace them as co-pastors.
